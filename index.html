The L.A.D.

(Look Alike Droid)

The L.A.D is a humanoid robot that copies your arm movements. A true follower spirit.

Team: Elisa Camacho, Mia Chevere, Zara Coakley, Mika Gazit, and Danny Kennen

OUR GOAL

L.A.D , or the Look-Alike Droid, is a robot that can copy a human's arm gestures using computer vision.
